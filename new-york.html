The North American movement was spectacular in the New York subway trains which were suddenly covered with names: Taki 183, Tracy 168, Akmy , Stay High 149, etc12 . In a few years , these " tags" ( signatures) have become real fonts . The goal of the North American Graffiti was initially to get the fame, the recognition of other graffiti taggers or their meaning by this that they exist. Very quickly , standardized styles and practices crystallize . Groups (called " posses ," " crews ," " squads " or " gangs "), as the city of New York has always known , form and allow graffiti artists to come together to perform spectacular feats , to add a collective name for their individual name, but also to compete between groups , peacefully or not.
In 1973, New York Magazine launches competition finest graffiti subway . A few years later the graffiti was severely repressed in the New York subway and began to spread on the walls of disadvantaged neighborhoods before ' spread to other major U.S. cities (Los Angeles , Chicago, Philadelphia , Houston ) and in various European cities : Paris , London , Berlin , Amsterdam and Barcelona in particular .
