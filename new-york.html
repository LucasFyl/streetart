In a few years, these «tags" have become real fonts. The goal of the North American Graffiti was initially to get the fame, the recognition of other graffiti taggers or their meaning by this that they exist. In 1973, New York Magazine launches the first competition finest graffiti subway. A few years later the graffiti was severely repressed in the New York subway and began to spread on the walls for our best pleasure!

Los Muros Hablan NYC

19th – 25th Aug

From August 19 – August 25, Los Muros Hablan, an international urban arts festival, gave a voice to silent, empty walls in East Harlem and the South Bronx. Presented by La Repuesta, a leading Puerto Rican cultural space, Los Muros Hablan NYC, featured a wonderfully eclectic group of global artists.
