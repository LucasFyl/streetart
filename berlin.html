Events in berlin

Street Art Meeting at YAAM

10 - 13 October

This Event is a absolut must for everyone who is interested in Street Art: “The Street Art Meeting 2013“! Organized by the “Urban Art Clash” collective the Street Art Meeting will take place from the 10. – 13.10.2013 at YAAM Berlin. Not just the incredible line up of Street Artist, but the chance to see these Street Artists, who usually operate under the cover of anonymity, live at work, makes that event to an absolut exception.

Stroke Art Fair

3 - 6 October

The Stroke Art Fair is back in Berlin! In case you joined the Stroke last year or just read about it on our Blog, you know that there is no way for us to get around this massive art event. After the Stroke Art Fair to place at the Postbahnhof Berlin it will proceed this year between the 3.10. and the 06.10.13 at the new location called “Alte Münze” in Berlin.

Street Art Attack

After Jimmy C and Bosso Fataka, it was time for the Berlin based Street Artist MYMO to show the people in Essen (and the rest of the world) what she and her art is all about. As a part of the “Street Art Attack” MYMO created the 3rd artwork, half painting, half installation at the nightclub Hotel Shanghai in Essen, where the people get the chance not just to have a look on an artwork but also on the artist herself and how the work has been developed until it was finnished.

Epic Street Art Battle

15th June

In conjunction with 48-Stunden-Neukoelln festivities the battle invites all audiences to witness the formation of street art and create a positive change of perspective.The first heat of artists will splash their paint skills at 18:00uhr and the final battle will kick off at 21:30uhr.Bertoldi’s Pop up Bar will be fully stocked with deliciously deadly shots and boozey delights to wet your whistle while the evening unfolds.
