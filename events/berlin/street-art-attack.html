Street Art Attack

This week the OCB Street Art Attack starts it´s tour in Berlin on Friday 12th of July at Ritter Butzke. First winner of the Street Art competition is the London based artist Jimmy C. He will paint a wall on Friday in front of the legendary club. Start of the Street Art Session will be at 10:00 a.m.. During the whole day the artist will make his masterpiece for the wall. The team of Street Art Berlin will be there also for all your questions.
