Street Art Meeting at YAAM

Streetartists from all over the globe will paint on huge walls inside and outside the legendary beach-club... straight at the East Side Gallery in the heart of Berlin. This long established beach-bar (complete with basketball court and half-pipe), huge walls and stages exudes the kind of chilled-out vibe that has become synonomous with the Caribbean. Whether you’re grinding sand between your toes, delighting in some lip-smacking chicken from the food stands or dangling your legs over the Spree as you quench your thirst with a Red Stripe, you’re sure to feel at home amongst the friendly crowd of regulars.
