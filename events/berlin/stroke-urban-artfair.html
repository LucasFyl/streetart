Artist of the week

Stroke Urban Artfair

From the 13th of September until the 19th of September the “Stroke Urban Art Fair” took place at „Postbahnhof„ in Berlin. The Stroke Urban Art Fair isn’t just the biggest, but one of the most interesting and international events that take place within an exhibition. Artist from Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Portugal, the Netherlands, Australia, Columbia, Chile, just to name a few countries, have been invited to this unique event. On first site, the Stroke is a platform for Galleries, Artists and Art Fans, who want to get in touch which each other. Beside the exhibition it was possible to see the Artist at work while doing live paintings or chat with them 1 to1 about their art.
