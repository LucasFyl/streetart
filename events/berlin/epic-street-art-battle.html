Epic Street Art Battle

Armed with aerosol cans, paintbrushes and marker pens, nine streets artists will battle live in front of the Retramp Gallery Saturday 15th June. In conjunction with 48-Stunden-Neukoelln festivities the battle invites all audiences to witness the formation of street art and create a positive change of perspective. The first heat of artists will splash their paint skills at 06:00p.m and the final battle will kick off at 09:30p.m.
