Artist presentation

Alias

Alias, the Berlin-based street artist was born in 1980 in a small town between Hamburg and Berlin.He dropped out of school and organized parties for some years with a couple of friends. Otherwise, he truly comes from a street background: skateboarding and Graffiti. This street artist got first introduced to street-art at the turn of the millennium while living in Hamburg where the sticker scene was really active. Nevertheless itâ€™s only since 2003, when he moved to Berlin that he started doing his own art and has since developed a unique street-art approach, becoming an important protagonist of this vivid urban art scene. Alias has constantly improved his stencil technique over the years and has now reached technical and aesthetic maturity thus always keeping a pinch of humor and grim realism in his works. Many of his posters and stickers produced with stencils and spray cans can be found in cities like Berlin, Hamburg, Amsterdam, Rome, Milan and also in Bristol.
