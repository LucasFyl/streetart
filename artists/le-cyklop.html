Artist presentation

Le cyklop

The Cyklop, French street artist, uses street furniture to practice his art. His preferred medium is the posts that line our streets curb. It gives them color, fantasy, and turns them into little people are watching their single blue eye, green, purple or orange. These bollards are the colors of African animals, fantastic creatures, always a surprising and fun that gives joy to our streets approach.
