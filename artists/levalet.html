Levalet

Professor of art during the day and street artist the night, Charles alias Leval Levalet seized the streets of Paris with paper collages and drawings in ink. Highly influenced by the paintings of Ernest Pignon Ernest and the works of his mentor's dark side,Banksy, the characters in black and white Levalet interact with the elements of place and often have objects directly grafted from street art scene as we like:. provocative, contemplative, philosophical player, kinetic, funny, romantic, poetic
